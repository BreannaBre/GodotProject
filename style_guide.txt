For visibility of direct descendants of main.tscn:
	"CanvasItem -> Ordering -> Z Index" should follow the following conventions:
	(please note that these are effective Z indices)
		0: background
		1: ship background
		2: ship rooms
		3: enemies
		4: players
		5: ship room decorations
